# Restaurant Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis (EDA)

## Overview
This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a restaurant dataset to understand customer behavior, spending patterns, and factors influencing tips. The objective is to derive actionable insights to support staffing optimization and revenue growth.

## Business Questions
- Which days and times have the highest customer traffic?
- How does group size affect the total bill amount?
- Does the total bill influence tipping behavior?
- Do customer attributes such as gender or smoking preference impact spending?

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

## Key Insights
- Weekend dinner hours represent the peak operational period.
- Group size shows a moderate positive relationship with total bill amount.
- Higher total bills are generally associated with higher tip amounts.

## Conclusion
The analysis highlights key customer and time-based patterns that can help improve operational planning and business decision-making.
